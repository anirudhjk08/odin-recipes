Chilly paneer

Description

Chilli Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with crispy fried paneer cubes tossed in a spicy, tangy, and slightly sweet sauce. It is commonly served as a starter or side dish and is enjoyed in both dry and gravy versions.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the paneer cubes until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for another minute.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the fried paneer cubes and toss to coat them in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot as an appetizer or side dish.
