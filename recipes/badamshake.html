Badam shake

Description

Badam shake is a delicious and nutritious drink made with almonds (badam), milk, and sugar. It is often flavored with cardamom and saffron, making it a popular choice for a refreshing beverage.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almonds (badam), soaked overnight
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2-3 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Drain the soaked almonds and blend them with a little milk to make a smooth paste.
  2. In a blender, combine the almond paste, remaining milk, sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron strands.
  3. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes if desired.
