Rasmalai

Description

Rasmalai is a popular Indian dessert made from soft paneer (chenna) balls soaked in sweetened milk flavored with cardamom and saffron. It is often garnished with nuts and served chilled.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice (for chenna)
  • 1 cup sugar (for syrup)
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil the milk in a pan and add lemon juice to curdle it. Strain the curds using a muslin cloth to make chenna.
  2. Knead the chenna until smooth and form small balls.
  3. In another pan, boil water with sugar to make a syrup. Add the chenna balls and cook for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a separate pot, boil milk with cardamom powder and saffron until it thickens slightly.
  5. Add the cooked chenna balls to the flavored milk and let them soak for a few hours.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts and serve chilled.
